About the Game

A peaceful puzzle experience designed around mindfulness

Quell Zen offers slow-paced puzzle gameplay where movement, atmosphere, and sound work together to create a deeply calming experience. You'll work through these beautifully designed levels, and each one gets a bit more interesting as you go. The game focuses on emotional relaxation, minimal distractions, and satisfying interaction flow. Every visual element should feel soft, balanced, and intentionally minimal.
